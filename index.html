About Us

The Broasted Story

At Broasted, we specialize in pressure-fried chicken that's crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Our secret blend of herbs and spices creates a unique flavor profile that keeps our customers coming back for more.

Founded in 2010, our mission has always been to serve the highest quality broasted chicken while providing exceptional customer service. We use only premium, fresh ingredients and prepare everything daily to ensure maximum flavor and freshness.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our food. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy a delicious meal together.
